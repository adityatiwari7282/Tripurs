  • Located in Karnataka, *Jogfalls* is the most spectacular waterfall of Western ghats. With a plunge of 830 feet, it is the second highest waterfall of India; highest waterfall being the Nohkalikai falls in Meghalaya. Want to add some spice in the itinerary? We will travel through ghats to reach this place and stay in a homestay surrounded by greenery and a garden of supari and cashew plantations at the backside. *Murudeshwar* is famous for the world's second tallest Shiva statue. The town lies on the coast of the Arabian Sea and the temple is visible from a long distance.
