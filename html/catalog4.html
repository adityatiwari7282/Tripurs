Hampi: The Heritage Trip

          ₹4,999.00

  • Hampi, recognized as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO, will take you back to ancient times by its magnificent architecture and the natural beauty around it. Situated at the beautiful bank of river Tungabhadra, Hampi has many historical temples and ancient monuments. Apart from its fascinating history, this place is also known for its scenic landscapes dotted with coconut trees and paddy fields.

  • *Day 1*:

  • 18:00- Start travelling to Hospet in an volvo bus from Mumbai. The bus has multiple pickup points - Andheri, Chembur, Vashi, Kharghar.

  • *Day 2*

  • - Reach Hospet
  • - Get the vibes of Hampi while crossing Tungabhadra river and a very scenic route with rice fields on both sides.
  • - Reach Sanapur and freshen up
  • – Exciting Coracle ride in Sanapur Lake. It is a round boat made of bamboo
  • - Visit Durga temple
  • - Visit Anjanadri Hills to watch the sunset while sipping your evening coffee. You will get an eagle view of the entire Hampi.
  • - Go Café-Hopping and enjoy the nightlife of Hampi

  • *Day 3*

  • - Get up early if you want to enjoy the morning sunrise and the birds
  • - After breakfast, we will explore the ancient temples and ruins of Vijayanagara Empire
  • Vithala Temple - it has the famous stone chariot, musical pillar.
  • Royal enclosure - lotus temple, old elephant stables and the beautiful garden
  • - Local South Indian meals on banana leaf
  • - Visit the huge Virupaksha temple dedicated to lord Shiva. Also we will visit famous elephant laxmi and see how she passes the blessings to devotees.
  • - Travel back to Hospet to board the bus.

  • *Day 4*

    • 08:30 - Reach Mumbai.
