Itenary:

Meghalaya-The Adobe of Clouds

          ₹24,000.00

  • Meghalaya, the abode of clouds, is famous for rainforests, root bridges, clean rivers, large unexplored caves and bamboo crafts. It has gorgeous waterfalls including the Nohkalikai falls i.e. the highest single plunge waterfall of India.

  • *Day 1*

  • Reach Guwahati. Preferred flight from Mumbai is Spicejet SG-8152 reaching Guwahati at 12:35. People from other cities can book accordingly.

  • *Day 2*

  • Mawryngkhang bamboo trek It is considered one of the thrilling treks of the North-East. Starting from a small hamlet in the East Khasi Hills, it takes places over a series of connected bamboo bridges that are laid out by the villagers over mountain ridges, farms, and roaring rivers.

  • *Day 3*

  • We will visit Dawki, a tiny hamlet on the border of India and Bangladesh. We will take a boat ride on the crystal clear waters of Umngot river. In Afternoon, we will go to Mawlynnong i.e. Asia’s cleanest village.

  • *Day 4*

  • Cherrapunji Sightseeing We will get to witness the 1000 metre plunge of Nohkalikhai Falls (includes an hour trek) Post lunch we will take a dip in the beautiful and clean step waterfalls of Wei Sawdong.(half hour trek)

  • *Day 5*

  • We will trek to the world famous double decker root bridge of Meghalaya. This bridge is formed by weaving the roots of trees which are over 1000 years old. The Rainbow Waterfall is a gorgeous waterfall, which is 2hours hike from the root bridge. In the afternoon, the sunlight from the west triggers a rainbow on these waterfalls.

  • *Day 6*

  • We trek back to Cherrapunji. In Afternoon we will visit the famous Arwah caves. In evening we can do shopping in the Shillong market famous for bamboo crafts.

  • *Day 7*

  • We will visit the famous Shillong peak which is the highest peak of Meghalaya offering stunning views of the city. Later, drive from Shillong to Guwahati airport. Preferred flight from Guwahati is Spicejet SG-8169 reaching Mumbai at 22:00
