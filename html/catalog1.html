  • About:

  • Arunachal, the land of dawn lit mountains is one of the least explored paradises of India. It shares international borders with Bhutan in the west, Myanmar in the east and is separated from China in the north by the disputed McMahon Line. With more than 60% area under forest cover, it has the most diverse flora and fauna and also known as the Orchid State of India.

  • 18:00 - Reach Guwahati and assemble at ISBT (final meeting point). Start traveling in bus (overnight journey)

  • Day2

  • 06:00 - Reach Bomdila and board a Jeep for Dirang. We will do the rest of the trip in a Jeep. 07:30 - Reach Dirang and rest till noon 11:00 - Explore the monastery, hot water spring and Sangti Valley. Overnight at Dirang.

  • Day3

  • 06:00 - Start traveling to Tawang. Explore Sela pass, Sela lake, Jang waterfall and the legendary Jaswant Singh memorial on the way 17:00 - Reach Tawang and rest.

  • Day4

  • Visit Tawang monastery and Buddha Stupa in the morning. In afternoon, we will travel to one of the most beautiful village i.e. Zemithang.

  • Day5,

  • Spend a relaxing day at Zemithang and enjoy the unique landscapes. We have various options like doing a small trek, visiting nearby monasteries, etc. In afternoon, we will travel to Lumla and stay there.

  • Day6

  • Visit the Taradevi temple at Lumla and then travel back to Tawang. We can visit the war memorial in evening.

  • Day7

  • Explore Bumla pass, Indo-China border, Sungester lake, PT Tso lake and many other high altitude lakes.

  • Day8

  • Travel to Bomdila early in the morning. Board the bus for Guwahati in late afternoon.

  • Day9

  • Reach Guwahati in the morning at approx 5AM. You can take flight from Guwahati after 8AM If you want to visit Kaziranga national park on your own then take return flight on Tuesday.
