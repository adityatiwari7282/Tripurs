Travel Course

Luxury travel style needs guidance, budget travel style needs planning but no budget travel style seems impossible to mostly everybody. Our founder and director Mr. Rajat Shukla has travelled in all the three styles very successfully. He has travelled in a shoetight budget when he was a student. He travelled in the most luxurious ways in his job as a journalist. And finally after being packed at home for months, he got out to travel few mountains of Maharashtra but that was not all, he continued his journey till Nepal and came back to visit All 28 States and 6 UTs of India absolutely without spending a single rupee for anything for nonstop 405 days.

This might seem impossible, but Mr. Rajat Shukla's journey in itself proves that its not just possible, but also very thrilling to travel without money/limited budget or luxury.

Tripurs presents, the ultimate course to be a traveller- Travel Infinity ♾️

Our course enables the student to gain knowledge about the place, its geography and its cultures.

Learning about the various conventional ways to commute from one place to another, find hosts by networking, finding volunteering opportunities/ creating one thyself. Connecting with tour operators, transport networks, hotels/hostels and tourist boards.

Through a combination of vocational and academic studies you learn about the relationships between travellers and the hosts throughout the globe.

The issues relating to sustainability and social responsibility in different regions.
